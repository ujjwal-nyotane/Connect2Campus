❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a bonafide certificate?
Visit the Academic Section (Admin Block, Room 5) with your ID card. You can also raise a ticket under "Certificate / Documents" and it will be couriered.
What is the minimum attendance to appear in exams?
You must maintain at least 75% attendance in each subject to be eligible to appear in semester exams. Shortage can be condoned in medical cases with valid proof.
How long does a leave application take to get approved?
Leave applications are typically reviewed within 2 working days by your class advisor. You will receive a notification on this portal once a decision is made.
How do I apply for a scholarship?
Scholarship applications open in August and January each year. Visit the Finance office or raise a ticket under "Scholarship" for guidance on eligibility and documents required.
What should I do if I face ragging?
Report immediately to the Anti-Ragging Committee at antirangging@connect2campus.edu or call the 24×7 helpline. All complaints are treated with strict confidentiality.
